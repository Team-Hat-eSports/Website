Ragnar aka Ragdoll

Rising from the ashes of countless battles, I stand as one of the last true War purists, honoring Auros, God of War, with every swing of my axe in tournaments and competitions. My playstyle is a versatile arsenal — mastering control with lizard swarms, unleashing aggro fury, and crafting combo decks like Relic, Carvings, and Azaiah with surgical precision. But my heart belongs to Phoenicia, the most breathtaking lady in the game — my bae — so balance chat nerds better keep her sacred name out of their filthy mouths, or face my wrath! As a cornerstone of Team HAT, I've taken Davuss and KorcJackson under my wing, inspiring them to ditch troll meme decks and rise to the occasion in tourneys. "War is my craft, Phoenicia is my muse, and victory is my destiny."
